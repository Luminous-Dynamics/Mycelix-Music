### 2. **Freemium** (30% of songs - Orange Badge)
**How it works**:
- **First 3 plays FREE** (per user, per song)
- After 3 plays: **$0.01 per stream**
- Try before you buy model

**Why it's better**:
- **Removes discovery friction** - users can sample music
- **Artist still gets paid** after free plays
- **Industry standard** (like Spotify's 30-day free trial)
- Sustainable for artists

**Example songs**:
- "Digital Dreams" by Nova Synthesis (12,847 plays, $89.23 earned)

- "Rise Up" by The Conscious Crew (28,901 plays, $260.19 earned)
