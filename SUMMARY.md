## The Problem

Traditional music streaming platforms impose a one-size-fits-all economic model on all artists:

- **Spotify:** $0.003 per stream, 90-day payout delays, opaque algorithms
- **Bandcamp:** 15% platform fee, download-only, limited streaming
- **Patreon:** Subscription-only, no per-play monetization

**Artists have no choice.** They must accept the platform's economic model or leave.

---

## Our Solution

**Mycelix Music** is the first decentralized music platform with **economic modularity** - each artist chooses or creates their own economic model.
